Tokens.
Tokens form the vocabulary of the Go. There are 4 classes:
  1 - indentifiers
  2 - keywords
  3 - operators and punctuation
  4 - literas

indentifiers - program entities such as variables and types
keywords - predeclared indentifiers (25)
operators and punctuation - +-=*&...,{}[] != etc
integer literals - sequence of digits representing an integer constant
